Greetings! My name is Svirkul Anton and I am writing to you with a motivation letter to apply for the Bachelor of Information Technology programme. I decided to make a small landing page with information about me, to avoid rather banal letters, to add some originality to my motivation letter, and to demonstrate some of my skills.

My Goals

For me personally, I consider the choice of a higher education institution to be an important thing in the life of every individual. That is why one of my main goals is to get a quality education and to be able to apply my knowledge in the field of computer technology to implement new ideas.

My Skills
